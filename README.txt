This project is meant to create a heart-shooting game.
When you open it, the first thing come up is a displaywindow with 2 buttons.
If you click play, game will start.
If you click quit, game will quit.
if choose to start the game, you will be the girl character in the game.
You can press up, down, left, right keys to move the girl, and press space to shoot hearts.
The boy will fall from the top of the window at random positions.
If your heart collide with the boy, you get 1 point.
Otherwise if you miss the boy and the boy falls out of the window, your life decreases by 1.
If you life reaches 0, the game is over.
And you can either restart or quit the game.
